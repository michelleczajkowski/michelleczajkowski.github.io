In the video below, you will watch - at 8x speed - how the text was written (length: three minutes).
Think about the problems you identified in step 2. Do you observe behavior that leads to these problems? What else do you notice about how this student goes about writing?"

To make a link between problems in the writing product, difficulties in the writing process, assessment and feedback, a chart like the one below can be used. The text is read first, and problems noted. Then, after watching the video of the writing process, notes are made about what difficulties were observed that lead to these problems (or note a lack of evidence). Afterwards, the implications for assessment can be considered: how could the (potential) difficulties be assessed? What would the (ideal) feedback form be for maximum utility by the test taker?
