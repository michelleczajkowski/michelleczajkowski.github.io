The Writing Product

Read through the text, an essay written by a high-level L2 Dutch (NT2) writer, as part of a diagnostic test of writing. The topic is familiar to the writer.

The Prompt

Beantwoord de volgende vraag in niet meer dan 300 woorden:

Hoe zouden we het schrijfproces kunnen diagnosticeren?

Je hebt 45 minuten om de opdracht te voltooien.
Je kunt elke online bron of website gebruiken die je wilt.

The Rating / Feedback Process

As you read, think about how you might assess this text. For diagnostic feedback, more detail is better, so you would likely use some kind of detailed analytical rubric.
