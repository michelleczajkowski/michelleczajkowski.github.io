Profile

Language assessment researcher and practitioner with experience in test development, validation, benchmarking, examiner training, test administration, and language teaching. My work focuses on diagnostic assessment, writing assessment, academic language readiness, and the interpretation and use of assessment results. I am especially interested in collaborative research and professional engagement that connects learners, teachers, test users, and assessment researchers across educational contexts.
