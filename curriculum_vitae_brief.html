Profile

I have experience in test development, teaching, and teacher training. I have worked with a range of testing organizations, including the British Council, ETS, and Cambridge English, and with language tests such as IELTS, TOEFL iBT, OET, KTE, Linguaskill, CNaVT, and DELNA/DELTA. I have also worked at the Language Testing Research Centre at the University of Melbourne. I am currently completing my PhD at Radboud University, where my research focuses on the validation of a post-entry language assessment in a bilingual university context. Alongside my research, I enjoy teaching and contributing to the wider language testing field, including through my role as ILTA Webinar Coordinator and conference organization.
